Continuous
Delivery

Component of DevOps

Continuous Delivery and Continuous Deployment are not the same. Continuous Delivery is the delivery of all code, as it is checked into source control, to development and test environments. Continuous Deployment is the frequent deployment of small bits of code to production but initiated by a human, not by automation. These are critical components of DevOps
